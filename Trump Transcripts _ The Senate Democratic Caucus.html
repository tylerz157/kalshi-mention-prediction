Trump Transcripts

In late May 2025, the White House quietly began removing official transcripts of President Trump’s public remarks from its website. While past administrations, including Trump’s own first term, maintained a comprehensive archive - the site now offers only a limited selection of videos and audio recordings.

This marks a clear rollback in transparency and public accessibility.

To fill the gap, Senate Democrats have committed to holding the administration accountable by ensuring Americans retain access to the president’s full remarks.

Courtesy of Roll Call’s Factbase, these transcripts are updated periodically and can be viewed below.
