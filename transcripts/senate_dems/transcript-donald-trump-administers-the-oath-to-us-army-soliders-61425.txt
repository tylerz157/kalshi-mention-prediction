TITLE: TRANSCRIPT: Donald Trump Administers the Oath to U.S. Army Soliders, 6.14.25

President Trump: I, state your name.

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Do solemnly swear –

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: That I will support and defend –

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: The Constitution of the United States of America –

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Against all enemies, --

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Foreign and domestic;

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: That I will bear true faith --

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: And allegiance to the same;

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: And that I will obey the orders

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Of the President of the United States

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: And the orders

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Of the officers appointed over me,

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: According to regulations

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: And the uniform code of military justice.

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: So help me God.

Soldiers: [Repeat the statement]

President Trump: Congratulations. Congratulations. Welcome to the United States Army and have a great life. Thank you very much. Have a great life. Thank you.
